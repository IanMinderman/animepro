Two boys try to bring their later mother back to life using socery, but fail. This is one of the first adult-oriented anime I ever watched. It taught me lessons about handling heavy topics such as death, slavery, and political conquest. I highly recommend it.

This anime is an amazing mystery. An associate professor of architectural engineering and the daughter of his mentor work together to solve the murders of a prominent artificial intelligence researcher and the director of the lab located on the island. It will shock you with its genius writing and romance!

Three orphans find out that they are being raiseed to be eaten by monster in the outside world. Upon learning the truth, they must pretend everything is fine as to not alert their carer, whom they call "Mother". You'll be on the edge of your seat as they plan their escape while outsmarting Mother!

This is a typical 'I woke up in a fantasy world' anime, but with an exception. The main character can't die. He's infinitely reincarnated. This leads to thrilling moments and a lot of what ifs. I like this anime because it has a good balance of action, theory, and romance. Watch it!
